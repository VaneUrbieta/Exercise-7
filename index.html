Clean Girl

minimalist, elegant, and effortless

White has always been a staple of the clean-girl aesthetic, since we’re used to associating it with neat and polished looks. Having this jacket as your top layer will completely tie the outfit together.

Off-the-shoulder pieces keep the look clean but also show that you care about looking elegant, which is an essential part of the clean-girl aesthetic. Black and white are the main colors found in every clean-girl wardrobe.

Washed denim jeans bring out the effortless side of the look. Even though everything feels well put together, the jeans make it seem like this is just your natural, everyday style.

Hair and makeup are an essential part of this look. The 'no-makeup' makeup style actually originated from this aesthetic—appearing natural and effortless while still looking elegant, especially when paired with a sleek, pulled-back bun.
